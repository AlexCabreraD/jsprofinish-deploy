Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common inquiries about our services, pricing, timeframes, and project steps.

What services do you offer?

We offer comprehensive basement finishing and remodeling services. From custom design and structural work to electrical, plumbing, and finishing touches, we handle every step to transform your basement into a beautiful, functional space.

Where do you operate?

JS ProFinish proudly serves homeowners and businesses across Utah, including but not limited to Salt Lake City, Ogden, Provo, and surrounding areas. Our team is dedicated to delivering top-quality basement finishing and remodeling services throughout the state.

How long does it take?

The duration of the project depends on the complexity and size of the basement. We strive to complete the work within the agreed-upon timeframe. Get in touch and we can provide a rough estimate.

Can I customize my basement?

Yes, you can customize your basement according to your preferences and needs. We offer a wide range of options for flooring, lighting, walls, ceilings, and other features to create a personalized space that suits your style.

How much does basement finishing cost?

The cost of basement finishing varies depending on the size of the basement, the scope of the project, and the materials and features chosen. We provide personalized quotes based on your specific requirements. Contact us for a free estimate.
